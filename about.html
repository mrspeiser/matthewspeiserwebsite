Matthew Speiser

Full-Stack Web Developer

Matthew Speiser is a new full-stack web developer with a passion for programming. A Los Angeles local, born in Santa Monica, he has been on the computer since adolescence. He is a quick learner and is excellent at following direction. Since a young age he has always been curious and shown the need to be creative. However, his technical and logical skills were not developed properly throughout puberty; battling Tourette Syndrome while taking medication.

Fast forwarding to today, he is brighter, and more stable than ever. No longer on standard perscription medication he is a user, not abuser, of medical cannabis. Although unable to finish college due to unfortunate family circumstances, he found ways to keep learning. A certified Mac user and certified integration user, he excels working with the Mac platform and terminal. He can fix screens, replace keyboards, replace hard drives, and all other types of general computer repair/upgrades

Matthew's computer coding journery started in 2015. He had always thought about coding, but never thought he would be smart enough to understand it. He tried some free online courses at codeacademy.com and eventually finished multiple courses. After completing the courses he browsed youtube videos and would teach himself the basics of how to set up basic html and css pages. Once he started to get the hang of basic html and css, he was hooked

Currently employed full-time in an customer service position, Matthew codes every single day once he gets home. He has completed "The Web Developer Bootcamp" and "The Complete Web Developer Course 2.0" on Udemy.com. Furthermore, he has been taking other courses online to satisfy his craving for development knowledge. He has an excellent understanding of all web system technologies and how they fit together. He is not in a top tier of programmers yet, but he can figure out working solutions in Javascript, Nodejs, PHP, and other languages

As far as Matthew knows he will continue to code everyday, improve, learn, and create.
